The upgrades that actually help tiny miners

Lottery miners do not need a luxury setup. They need steady power, steady cooling, and a way to measure whether a change helped.

Power supply and cable

A weak supply or thin cable can make a good tune look unstable. If voltage drops under load, fix power delivery before blaming the chip.

Power meter

A meter turns guessing into measurement. It helps compare watts, hashrate, heat, and electricity cost per setting.

Cooling and airflow

Better airflow can let the same voltage and frequency run more consistently. Focus on clean intake, exhaust path, and room temperature.

What to skip first

Do not spend big on cosmetic upgrades before the basics. The first useful purchases should make stability measurable.
