Choose the miner that matches your goal

The right lottery miner depends on whether you want learning, visible hashrate, low power, quiet desk appeal, or the cheapest possible experiment.

Bitaxe

Best for hands-on tuning, real ASIC learning, and visible terahash-class lottery mining. It is still wildly unlikely to find a block, but it feels like a real miner.

NerdMiner

Best for education and desk novelty. It teaches the idea, but the hashrate is not comparable to a Bitaxe.

USB stick miners

Best for hobbyists who enjoy tinkering with host devices, hubs, cooling, and compact setups.

Before buying

Run the expected hashrate through the calculator. If the odds still feel fun after seeing the math, the device may be worth it as a hobby tool.
