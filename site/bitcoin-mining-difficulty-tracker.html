Why this can bring in new readers

Difficulty adjustment searches happen on a schedule. People look up the next retarget, see a percent move, then wonder what it means for their miner. Block Odds Lab should be waiting there with the practical answer: rerun the odds with current difficulty before buying, tuning, or posting old screenshots.

How to use the tracker

  • If difficulty is estimated to rise, your odds get worse unless your hashrate rises too.
  • If difficulty is estimated to fall, your odds improve slightly, but the lottery-mining reality check still applies.
  • When the retarget is close, rerun the calculator instead of trusting old results.
  • Use the tracker link in comments when someone asks why their old Bitaxe or NerdMiner odds changed.
