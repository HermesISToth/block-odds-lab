Gamma buyer sanity check

If the Gamma link came from a short video, comment, or shop page, run the expected-time math before treating it like an income machine. The useful question is not whether a Gamma can find a block; it is whether the measured hashrate, watts, and cost make sense for your hobby, learning, or content goal.

Compare one Gamma against two Gammas, then compare that result against a cheaper stability upgrade like airflow, a cleaner PSU, or a meter. If the odds gain barely moves, the better purchase may be the part that makes your current miner more reliable.

The Gamma can be a great hobby miner and a strong learning tool. The calculator keeps that separate from pretending it is a predictable income machine.
