Odds

Every hash is a ticket, but the raffle is enormous

Bitcoin lottery mining is simple to understand and hard to emotionally accept. A tiny miner can find a block, but the average wait can be thousands of years.

The rough formula

The probability per hash is approximately one divided by difficulty times 2^32. More hashrate means more attempts. Higher difficulty means a harder target. The proof-of-work and target mechanics are documented in the Bitcoin Developer Guide.

Expected time is not a schedule. A miner can get lucky early, or never find a block at all.

Why the calculator matters

The calculator turns a tempting hashrate number into daily, monthly, and yearly odds. That keeps upgrades grounded in reality.
