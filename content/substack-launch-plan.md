# Block Odds Lab Substack Launch Plan

Working publication name: Block Odds Lab Notes

Positioning: sober math for Bitcoin lottery miners.

Primary job: turn BOL articles, calculator pages, and difficulty changes into a recurring distribution channel that sends readers back to `blockoddslabs.com`.

Canonical home remains: https://blockoddslabs.com/

## Publication Setup

- Name: Block Odds Lab Notes
- Short description: Honest odds, tuning notes, and decision math for Bitcoin lottery miners.
- About line: Block Odds Lab helps hobby miners understand solo block odds before buying, tuning, or upgrading gear.
- Suggested categories: Bitcoin, mining, hardware, hobby engineering
- Paid subscriptions: off at launch
- Custom domain: skip at launch

## Editorial Rules

- Never imply lottery mining is likely to make money.
- Use plain-English odds, expected time, yearly chance, power cost, and practical tradeoffs.
- Every post must link back to Block Odds Labs. Use at least one relevant calculator/article link, and include the canonical site link when the post can naturally support it.
- Prefer a short link block near the end: canonical home, main calculator, and the most relevant topic page.
- Keep posts useful even if the reader never buys anything.
- Use affiliate disclosures once affiliate links exist.
- Avoid generic Bitcoin news unless it changes mining odds or hobby miner decisions.
