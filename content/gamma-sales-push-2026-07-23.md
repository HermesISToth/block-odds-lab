## Substack Note Draft

The Bitaxe Gamma pitch gets better when it gets more honest.

Show the hardware first. Then ask the buyer to run the boring inputs:

- stable hashrate after real uptime
- watts at the wall
- current Bitcoin difficulty
- one board vs. two boards
- whether cooling or power stability should come before more hardware

That keeps the Gamma in the right category: a small ASIC that can teach real mining mechanics and make good content, not a predictable income box.
