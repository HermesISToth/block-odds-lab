# New weekend challenge: run the odds before chasing the $200K Bitaxe story

The $200K small-miner jackpot story is exactly why lottery mining is fun.

It is also exactly why the math needs to be visible before anyone buys another board, pushes a hotter tune, or treats a Bitaxe Gamma like a profit machine.

So the July 18-20 Block Odds Lab weekend challenge is simple:

1. Pick one setup: Bitaxe Gamma, another Bitaxe, NerdMiner, USB stick miner, or solo-pool toy.
2. Enter your real hashrate and watts.
3. Save the yearly chance, expected time, expected BTC, power cost, and hardware payback.
4. Change one variable: stable tune, pushed tune, second board, cheaper power, better cooling, or a different miner.
5. Share the before/after result with one sentence: did the change actually move the odds enough to justify it?

The point is not to kill the fun. The point is to keep the fun honest.
