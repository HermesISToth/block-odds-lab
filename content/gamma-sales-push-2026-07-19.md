## BitcoinTalk / Forum Reply Draft

For anyone looking at a Bitaxe Gamma after seeing short-form videos, I think the useful comparison is one board vs. two boards, not "can this make predictable income?"

A second board can double your hashrate, but it does not turn a lottery miner into a predictable return profile. The inputs I would check first:

- stable hashrate after real uptime
- watts at the wall
- current difficulty
- yearly solo block chance
- whether cooling or power stability would help before more hardware
